EMP – Chrome Extension

⌘ + Enter to comment and automatically unsubscribe.
When commenting on Facebook, I'm often giving feedback or advice, answering a question, or making a terrible pun. Commenting automatically subscribes me to be notified of all further activity on a post, which is usually something I don't want. EMP is a simple chrome extension that fixes this.

Kickback – LA Hacks 2015

Effortless, fire-and-forget Amazon refunds when prices change.
Amazon has a policy stating that if a product you buy from them has a lower price within a week of your purchase, you are entitled to a refund of the difference. Claiming is a hassle, but Kickback does all the legwork for you.


Guardian – MHacks 6

Get out of unsafe situations quickly and easily.
Guardian calls an Uber to a calculated location ahead of you based on your pace and ETA. As you're walking, Guardian will prompt you with haptic directions to get you to your Uber. All along, an emergency contact is notified of each stop. No oblivious map-staring required.


Hamilton – Battle of the Hacks

Your smart personal finance assistant.
Hamilton allows you to query all of your finance information through a dead-simple NLP chat interface. It's able to handle complex questions, and can help you gain an insight into how you've been spending you money.


Tone – Bitcamp 2016

Be your best self when writing emails.
Over text, it can be easy to come off the wrong way. We wanted to solve this in an unobtrusive way, so you can sound as good over email as you do in live conversation. While writing an email, Tone analyzes your message in real time to let you know if you're sounding too self-centered or aggressive, or very open and agreeable. Tone provides a helpful 'language graph' at the top of your compose window, visually breaking down what you’ve written.
